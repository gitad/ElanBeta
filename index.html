A LITTLE ABOUT ELAN

Energy , Style And Enthusiasm
ELAN, a mesmerizing unification of Energy, Style and Enthusiasm is all set to hold Hyderabad spellbound once again, with its seventh edition being launched at the start of 2017.
This Annual Inter­College “Techno­Cultural” festival of IIT Hyderabad promises a plethora of events to stupefy and ensorcell both epicurean and boisterous tastes alike. Since its dawn in 2009 by dreadfully talented students, Elan has left a special memory in the hearts of each and every participant, visitor and organizer.

Keeping up with the sucessful 3­day model, ELAN 2017 is well stocked with events from the Cultural (Culti), Technical (Techy), Informal (Infi) and Literary (Litr) domains. Each event is finely crafted to reflect the avid ardor in appreciating and experiencing genuine inspiration in art, music and technology that is the culture of Elan.
Elan is an adventure filled with Energy , Tashaan and Enthusiasm.
Its an odyssey worth enduring !!!.

Hospitality


Elan is fast on its way to becoming one of the highlights of the year, of the city of Hyderabad and all of India. In this celebration of human skill, creativity and pure undiluted awesomeness, it is vital that all participants be able to give it their all, for all the events.

We assure you that your short stay with us will be fun, secure and comfortable. Our team will be available round the clock to make you feel at home to address your every need.

Accomodation

Accommodation during the Workshops and Festival (20-22nd jan, 2012) will be provided. Mattresses, pillows, buckets and mugs will be provided once accommodation is confirmed.

Rules

Dear All, This is to inform you about the rules to be followed in the IITH campus, to make Elan-12 a hassle-free and fun filled experience for everyone.

* Kindly carry your college ID card and IITH Registration card in the campus.
* Any kind of misbehaviour will not be tolerated and will be dealt with, strictly.
* Frisking is done for security reasons. Kindly cooperate.
* Security personnel are not responsible for any of your personal belongings.
* Security personnel reserve the right to refuse your entry at any point of time if they deem it unsafe.
* Kindly cooperate with ELAN-12 team.
* The accommodation fee and deposit may be paid at the time of registration, deposit will be refunded at the time of check out if the registration card is submitted back.
* The students are advised not to keep large amounts of cash or valuables in the room. The student is responsible for the safety of his/ her belongings.
* Any damage/breakage to hostel property will be charged to the occupants of the room/ block with a fine. Disciplinary action will also be initiated.
* Substance abuse, consumption of alcohol and smoking or chewing of tobacco and its related products is strictly banned in the campus.
* Water should be carefully used and not wasted.
* Keep the campus clean.
* The student has to deposit the room key at the registration desk when you are going out for long duration.
* Coupons for food will be given only to individuals having registration card.
